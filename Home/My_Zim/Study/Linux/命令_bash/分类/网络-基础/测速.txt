====== 测速 ======
Created Monday 03 December 2018

speedtest-cli
https://www.cmsky.com/linux-speedtest-cli/
root@ubuntu:~# wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sivel/speedtest-cli/master/speedtest.py  获取speedtest-cli
root@ubuntu:~# chmod +x speedtest.py
root@ubuntu:~# mv speedtest.py /usr/bin/  移动到/usr/bin/，这样就不用每次都输入完整路径
root@ubuntu:~# speedtest.py  运行
