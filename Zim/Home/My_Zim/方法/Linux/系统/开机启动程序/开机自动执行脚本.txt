====== 开机自动执行脚本 ======

## 把想要开机启动的脚本存放到 /etc/init.d/
## `sudo chmod +x /etc/init.d/xxxxx.sh`
## `sudo chown root:root /etc/init.d/xxxxx.sh `
## `sudo update-rc.d xxxxx.sh defaults`
